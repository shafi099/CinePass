Welcome to Cinepass, the ultimate destination for movie ticket booking! This webpage is designed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to provide you with a seamless and immersive movie experience.

You can select the date, time, and seat(s) of your choice, and the webpage automatically updates the price and total cost of your ticket(s). You can make the payment using a secure payment gateway, and your ticket(s) will be instantly delivered to your email.

The webpage has a responsive design that adapts to different screen sizes, making it easy to book tickets on your desktop, laptop, tablet, or mobile device.
